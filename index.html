What It Does

Turn noisy feed traffic into clear server channels and personal daily digests.

Newsletter Bot gives you two connected experiences: story-first server channels for community updates and a personal daily DM newsletter for individual readers. Both are built around grouped stories, source clarity, and practical controls instead of raw link dumps.

  • Server owners run up to 3 free channels per server, each with grouped stories, presets, role pings, and channel-specific settings.
  • Each free channel supports up to 2 custom feeds and refresh choices at 30, 45, or 60 minutes.
  • Users can subscribe to personal daily DM newsletters with source, topic, delivery day/time/timezone, and saved-story controls.

FAQ

Common questions from server owners and users.

Is the free version actually useful for daily use?

Yes. Free includes up to 3 server channels, up to 2 custom feeds per channel, grouped stories, source browsing, role pings, presets, and one personal DM newsletter delivery per day.

What is a linked premium server?

Premium is attached to a user subscription first. If that user manages a server, they can link a premium slot to it so that server gets premium channel limits and timing controls.

What does Reader unlock?

Reader ($2.99/month) upgrades personal newsletters only: 2 deliveries per day, 25 tracked topics, 10 custom personal feeds, digest up to 35 stories, 250 saved stories, and 2 profiles.

What does Reader Plus add?

Reader Plus ($5.99/month) includes Reader perks and adds 1 linked premium server with up to 7 channels, up to 10 custom feeds per channel, refresh as low as 10 minutes, quiet hours, posting windows, channel cloning, and tracked-topic instant alerts.

What does Reader Pro add?

Reader Pro ($9.99/month) includes Reader Plus perks and raises linked premium servers to 3 slots, linked channel capacity to 10 per server, refresh as low as 5 minutes, very high custom feed capacity, and larger personal limits.

Which add-ons are available?

You can buy +1 Linked Premium Server ($2.99 one-time), Extra Feed Pack ($1.99 one-time), Founding Supporter ($4.99 one-time), and Tip Jar support purchases.

Can users choose exactly when DM newsletters arrive?

Yes. Personal daily DM newsletters support delivery day, time, timezone, sort mode, digest focus, tracked topics, favorite/muted sources, and saved stories.

Ready to run a cleaner Discord news experience?

Invite Newsletter Bot, launch cleaner server channels, and let users opt into personal daily DM newsletters. Start free, then upgrade to Reader, Reader Plus, or Reader Pro when you need more personal depth or linked server power.
