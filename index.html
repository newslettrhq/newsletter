What It Does

Turn noisy feed traffic into clear server channels and personal daily digests.

Newsletter Bot gives you two connected experiences: story-first server channels for community updates and a personal daily DM newsletter for individual readers. Both are built around grouped stories, source clarity, and practical controls instead of raw link dumps.

  • Server owners configure up to 3 channels with presets, role pings, and custom feed support.
  • Readers browse grouped stories with multiple sources in one clean thread.
  • Users can subscribe to personal daily DM newsletters with topic, source, and schedule controls.

FAQ

Common questions from server owners and users.

How many channels can a server run by default?

Most servers can run up to 3 news channels by default, each with its own delivery and formatting behavior.

What do members see after setup is complete?

Members see grouped story updates in server channels, and they can optionally subscribe to their own personal daily DM newsletter.

What can users control in personal daily DM newsletters?

Users can choose categories, manage sources, follow tracked topics, favorite or mute sources, and save stories for later.

Can users choose when their DM newsletter is delivered?

Yes. Users can pick delivery days, time, timezone, sort mode, and digest focus mode for their personal daily digest.
