FAQ

Common questions from server owners and users.

How many channels can a server run by default?

Most servers can run up to 3 news channels by default, each with its own delivery and formatting behavior.

Can members get personal news without affecting channel settings?

Yes. Users can run /getnews and maintain personal preferences independently of server channel configuration.

Does the bot post every single article from every feed?

No. The bot is built around story grouping and cleaner organization so channels stay readable and focused on major developments.

Can server owners manage source naming and quality?

Yes. Owner-side tools include source cleanup, title override controls, and feed review concepts for long-term channel quality.
