Privacy Policy

Effective April 4, 2026. This policy explains what data Newsletter Bot processes, why it is processed, and what controls users and server admins have.

Scope

This policy applies to use of Newsletter Bot inside Discord, including personal `/getnews` usage and server channel configurations.

Data We Store

  • Discord user, server, role, and channel IDs needed for configuration and delivery.
  • Bot preferences such as channel presets, posting behavior, topic preferences, and feed selections.
  • Custom feed URLs and source configuration provided by admins or users.
  • Operational metadata such as timestamps and error logs for reliability, abuse prevention, and debugging.

Data We Do Not Sell or Profile

Newsletter Bot does not sell personal data and is not built for ad profiling. Data is used to run and improve bot behavior, not for advertising audiences.

How Data Is Used

  • Deliver grouped stories and source-aware updates to configured channels or personal flows.
  • Maintain personal preferences and server-level posting rules.
  • Support feed health checks, source cleanup, and owner-side curation workflows.
  • Improve ranking and organization quality through structured product signals.

Retention

Configuration data is retained while the bot is actively used. Data tied to removed servers or users is deleted during cleanup workflows. Operational logs are retained only as long as needed for reliability and security operations.

Sharing and Processors

Data is not sold to third parties. Service operation depends on Discord platform infrastructure and hosting providers that process data only to run the bot.

Security

Reasonable technical and operational safeguards are used to protect stored configuration data and credentials. No service can guarantee absolute security, but protection and review procedures are maintained.

Your Controls

  • Users can request deletion of their stored bot configuration data.
  • Server admins can remove the bot and stop processing for that server.
  • Users and admins can update preferences and feed settings at any time.

Requests can be submitted through the support server.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated as the product evolves. Material changes are reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
